Unified Payments

UX Design Case Study — Unified Payments Demo

Overview

This was my first project as Design Lead at Global Payments. Product asked for a checkout demo to showcase REST API products packaged under 'Unified Payments'. The goal was to replace an older static demo with a richer knowledge hub and demo experience.

My role

Design Lead – managing the project, driving stakeholder engagement and creative design.

Timeline

June to September 2022; Internal Beta Launch May 2023

Design Thinking

Collaborated with stakeholders, ran initial user research and discovery sessions to understand Sales and CSM needs, then iterated through sketches, prototypes and user testing.

Beta release & future

Launched internally at the Dublin Offsite; created launch assets and identified future growth areas for the demo (value adds, API Explorer, vertical messaging).
