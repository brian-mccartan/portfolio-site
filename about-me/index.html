About Me

–

I specialise in leading design strategy and execution for complex e-commerce and payment experiences. My role spans setting vision, driving alignment with product and business goals, and ensuring high standards of usability, accessibility and design excellence. I collaborate across functions, mentor design teams, and create solutions informed by research, prototyping and systems thinking.

I hold a first class honours Master of Arts in User Experience and Service Design from Munster Technological University, a Professional Diploma in UX Design from the UX Design Institute and Glasgow Caledonian University, and a BA (Hons) in Visual Communications from Crawford College of Art & Design.

I'm also a musician. (More on that below!).
